Cloud Computing

In the fast-changing technology environment, Cloud adoption is at the heart of transforming IT to be more relevant, agile and deliver more value to the business. At the core, it is the applications and data that are enabled by the Cloud that deliver function to the business, drive innovation, streamline operations and reduce cost. Simple Solutions AI offers certified cloud architects and engineers with years of experience in helping clients migrating from on-prem to cloud environments, by defining right cloud strategy as relevant to client business needs and a roadmap for journey to the cloud (J2C) in a phased manner that minimizes the disruption to the business and maximize return on investment. Our consultants utilize an standard J2C framework which involves actionable processes for an effective cloud adoption and utilization. The core of our J2C framework uses the 7R methodology to determine the disposition of each legacy application, and roadmap to modernize applications.

Key phases of our J2C framework include:

  • Application disposition questionnaire
  • Cloud migration planning – application inventory, schedule, cost
  • Cloud migration design – detailed plan and sequencing of activities
  • Cloud migration execution – implementation and validation
