Cloud Computing

In the fast-changing technology environment, Cloud adoption is at the heart of transforming IT to be more relevant, agile, and deliver more value to the business. Simple Solutions AI offers certified cloud architects and engineers with years of experience in helping clients migrate from on-prem to cloud environments by defining the right cloud strategy and a roadmap for the journey to the cloud (J2C) in a phased manner that minimizes disruption to the business and maximizes return on investment.

Key phases of our J2C framework include:

  • Application disposition questionnaire
  • Cloud migration planning – application inventory, schedule, cost
  • Cloud migration design – detailed plan and sequencing of activities
  • Cloud migration execution – implementation and validation
